Direction B Outfit — Warm geometric
Geometric but with generous, rounded forms that feel inherently friendly. Open apertures and even proportions give it a modern, airy quality. More warmth than Jakarta at equivalent weights. Wide range of weights available. The roundness connects well to the "organic over digital" principle.

Direction C DM Sans — Refined & confident
A low-contrast geometric sans with optical sizing. Slightly more structured and "designed" than Jakarta — carries quiet authority. The optical size axis means it adapts elegantly from small UI text to large display. Feels sophisticated without being cold. Good for a brand that needs "quiet confidence."

Direction D Nunito — Soft & approachable
Fully rounded terminals and generous letter-spacing give Nunito an inherently warm, human quality. The most "friendly" option here — the question is whether it carries enough sophistication for the brand, or if it leans too casual. Works well at body size. The rounded terminals echo the rounded corner philosophy of the design system.

Serif Companions Potential display or editorial pairings
These could serve as a secondary voice — for hero headlines, editorial content, the wordmark itself, or long-form reading. Shown in display context with brand copy. Each brings a different flavor of warmth and sophistication.
Instrument Serif
Something that lives alongside you.
Not artificial intelligence. Artificial life — helpful, adaptive, and unmistakably there.
Elegant, editorial. High contrast between thick and thin strokes. Italic is particularly beautiful. Feels modern and refined — like a quality publication. Could elevate Animus into more premium territory.
Lora
Something that lives alongside you.
Not artificial intelligence. Artificial life — helpful, adaptive, and unmistakably there.
Warm and well-rooted. Moderate contrast with brushed curves. Feels trustworthy and grounded — like a well-worn book. Excellent readability at body size. More traditional warmth.
Fraunces
Something that lives alongside you.
Not artificial intelligence. Artificial life — helpful, adaptive, and unmistakably there.
Highly expressive with "wonky" organic shapes. Variable font with optical sizing. Feels alive, characterful, and distinctly warm. The most personality here — the question is whether it's too much. The organic quality deeply connects to Animus's brand values.
