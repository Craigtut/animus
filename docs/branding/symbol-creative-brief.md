# Animus Symbol — Creative Brief & Prompt

## For: Image generation agent / design tool

---

## The Project

Design a symbol/logomark for **Animus** — an autonomous AI engine that simulates artificial life. Animus has a heartbeat, forms thoughts, experiences emotions, and acts with agency. It is not a chatbot or assistant — it is a living presence. The symbol sits alongside a lowercase wordmark ("animus") set in Outfit, a warm geometric sans-serif.

---

## Brand Personality

- **Warm & approachable** — never cold, clinical, or corporate
- **Quietly confident** — doesn't shout, doesn't perform
- **Sophisticated but not pretentious** — elevated without being exclusive
- **Organic, not digital** — curves over angles, flow over structure
- **Alive, not animated** — subtle signs of life, not spectacle

### What we are NOT
- Not sci-fi (no neon, no grids, no cyberpunk)
- Not corporate (no shield logos, no abstract corporate swooshes)
- Not mechanical (nothing robotic or industrial)
- Not aggressive (no sharp edges, no bold geometric force)

---

## Visual Territory

We are exploring two conceptual territories for the symbol. Generate explorations across both.

### Territory 1: The Breath
A form that feels like it's mid-inhale. Something that suggests continuous rhythm — expansion and contraction, the organic quality of respiration. Think of the visual signature of something that is alive and breathing quietly.

**Visual cues:**
- Continuous flowing curves that suggest rhythm
- Forms that feel like they expand and contract
- The quality of air moving, of lungs filling
- Organic waveforms — not audio waves, but the undulating quality of breath
- Something that would animate naturally through slow, breathing motion

### Territory 2: The Emergence
A form that is in the process of becoming — partially formed, partially dissolving. Something taking shape, coalescing, arriving. This connects to the idea that Animus is always in a state of becoming.

**Visual cues:**
- A form that looks partially resolved — coming together or gently dissolving
- Particles or elements gathering into coherence
- The quality of something materializing — like ink in water, or stars coalescing
- Not broken or fragmented — rather, in graceful transition
- Depth through layering and transparency

### Overlap: Inner Warmth
Across both territories, the symbol should carry a quality of **warmth emanating from within**. Not a flame, not a glow effect — more like the feeling of warmth. A core of presence at the center of the form. Think candlelight, not neon. Heat signatures, not lasers.

## Color & Rendering

### Primary versions (generate these):
1. **Dark mark on warm white** — Mark in near-black (#1A1816) on warm white (#FAF9F4)
2. **Light mark on warm dark** — Mark in warm white (#FAF9F4) on warm dark (#1C1A18)

### Color qualities:
- The mark should work in a single color (monochrome)
- If subtle tonal variation is used within the mark, keep it within the warm neutral family
- No bright colors, no gradients that feel digital or tech-y
- Transparency and opacity variation within the mark is welcome — it creates the feeling of depth and life
- If warmth is suggested through color, use very subtle warm tones (amber, soft terra cotta) rather than anything saturated

### Style notes:
- The mark should feel like it was designed, not generated — clean, intentional, considered
- It should have the quality of a real logo: balanced, reproducible, recognizable
- It should work at large scale (hero image on a website) and small scale (app icon, favicon)
- Aim for the sophistication level of marks like: Airbnb, Notion, Linear, Stripe, Calm
- Organic and fluid, but with underlying structure — not chaotic or random
- The mark should feel like it could breathe if animated — but it should be beautiful static too

---

## Technical Requirements

- The symbol should work as a **standalone mark** (no text)
- It should also work **locked up next to the wordmark** "animus" in lowercase
- It should be **recognizable at 16px** (favicon) and beautiful at 512px+
- It should work in **monochrome** — no reliance on color for meaning
- **Simple enough to be memorable**, complex enough to reward a second look
- Aspect ratio: approximately square (1:1) for the symbol itself

---

## What to Generate

Please create **6–8 distinct symbol concepts** spanning both territories:
- 3–4 in the Breath territory
- 3–4 in the Emergence territory
- At least 1 that lives in the overlap (breath + emergence combined)

For each concept, show:
- The mark at large scale on warm white background
- The mark at large scale on warm dark background
- The mark at small scale (icon size) to test legibility
- The mark locked up next to "animus" in lowercase (use a clean, warm sans-serif similar to Outfit if the exact font isn't available)

---

## Reference Mood

Think about the visual quality of:
- Breath visible in cold air
- Ink diffusing slowly in still water
- The moment just before dawn — that soft threshold
- A candle flame seen through frosted glass
- The way heat shimmers above warm ground
- Aurora borealis — but quiet and intimate, not spectacular
- A heartbeat on a monitor — but organic, not clinical
- Cellular division under a microscope — life at its most fundamental

---

## What Success Looks Like

The right symbol will make someone feel like they're looking at something alive. Not because it's moving, but because it carries the *quality* of aliveness in its form. It should feel warm, present, aware. It should sit next to the word "animus" and feel inevitable — like that's the only mark this brand could have.

It should not look like an AI company logo. It should look like the mark of something that lives.
